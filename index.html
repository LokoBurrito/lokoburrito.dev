The End

After 7 years of going by LokoBurrito, i think its time to let it rest now.5

it's been a wild time using the name. I had a lot of fun, got to meet new people, run projects, host servers, create websites like this, and even start up businesses and shops. But recently ive come to terms that its time to retire and let go of it. ive been starting to notice that now im starting to grow away from what i really wanted the name to mean. I made the name to make another identity of myself that i could use to represent a silly, fun, and humorous version of myself. But now, im no longer like that. and im happy to realize that instead of sliding it under the rug and hiding from the truth.
