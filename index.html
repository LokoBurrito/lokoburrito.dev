The End

After 7 years of going by LokoBurrito, i think its time to let it rest now.5

it's been a wild time using the name. I had a lot of fun, got to meet new people, run projects, host servers, create websites like this, and even start up businesses and shops. But recently ive come to terms that its time to retire and let go of it. ive been starting to notice that now im starting to grow away from what i really wanted the name to mean. I made the name to make another identity of myself that i could use to represent a silly, fun, and humorous version of myself. But now, im no longer like that. and im happy to realize that instead of sliding it under the rug and hiding from the truth. Its been a good 7 years too. but the main reason im removing it is because of recent events that i will not go into full detail here. im glad about some things i did with the name, upset about what i did, and still sad about the things i didnt achieve with it. my internal debate started after Roblox banned my account becuase of some random file i was running on my computer was running in the background, giving me a unappealable ban. and it wiped off all of my progress. This was when i started getting distant from the name, when i realized that everything that i built, even my legacy, was wiped off forever. With Roblox support not helping with recovering my games, files, or even account, i came to terms that i would never see my legacy ever again. With the Loss of $2k US, i quit for a few months and left. pondering about what i would do next.
